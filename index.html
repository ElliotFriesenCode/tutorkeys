Tutorkeys is a not for profit with the goal of teaching as many people piano as possible.

The platform connects piano tutors to learners in a personal way where anyone can learn and acheive their goals as a pianist.

How it works

1. Learns log on and send in a question

2. Tutors answer the questions

Why learn? Learning music reduces anxiety, blood pressure, and pain as well as improves sleep quality, mood, mental alertness, and memory.
